Just like enchants name, you can reformat the level number of enchants.

Explaination:
  - The key in the section is the enchant level.
  - The value is the name you want to replace with that enchant.

```
enchants-level:
  '1': I
  '2': II
  '3': III
  '4': IV
  '5': V
  '6': VI
  '7': VII
  '8': VIII
  '9': IX
  '10': X
```
